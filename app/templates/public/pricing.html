Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I exceed my link limit? ▼
When you reach your monthly link limit, you'll be prompted to upgrade to a higher plan. Your existing links will continue to work normally, but you won't be able to create new ones until the next billing cycle or until you upgrade.
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time? ▼
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for Pro? ▼
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for the Pro plan. No credit card required. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with Gosha for any reason, contact us within 30 days of your purchase for a full refund.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
Can I use my own custom domain? ▼
Yes! Custom domains are available on the Pro and Enterprise plans. You can connect your own domain (e.g., go.yourbrand.com) and use it for all your shortened links.

Our team is here to help. Contact us and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
